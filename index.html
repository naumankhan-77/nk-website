Immigration services

Navigate your immigration with confidence.

Important notice: NK Recruitment provides immigration guidance, information and referrals only. We are not a licensed legal firm or solicitor. For complex legal matters, we refer clients to qualified immigration solicitors. All services are quoted on a case-by-case basis. First 30 minutes of consultation is free.

Visa Applications

Guidance on Irish, UK and European visa applications — work visas, student visas, tourist and long-stay visas.

Work Permit Support

We guide employers and employees through Critical Skills and General Employment Permit applications for Ireland.

Student Visas & Study Abroad

Expert guidance for students seeking to study in Ireland, the UK or Europe — including university placement and scholarships.

Family Reunification

Assistance with dependent and family visa applications to help your loved ones join you in Ireland or the UK.

IRP Registration

We help you prepare for Irish Residency Permit (IRP) registration, including all required documentation and booking.

Immigration Stamp Guidance

Clear guidance on Stamp 1, 1G, 2, 3, and 4 — eligibility, rights and how to transition between stamp conditions.

Document Authentication

Support with document translation, apostille certification, and Garda vetting requirements for immigration purposes.

Employer Sponsorship

We assist employers in sponsoring international workers and navigating CSEP and GEP permit requirements in Ireland.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Who we are

NK Recruitment is a recruitment and immigration consultancy based at 19 Watergate Street, Navan, Co. Meath, Ireland. We are committed to protecting your personal data in line with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Data Protection Acts 1988–2018.

2. What data we collect

  • Name, email address, phone number and location
  • CV / résumé and employment history
  • Nationality and right-to-work information
  • Immigration status and document details (where applicable)
  • Website usage data via cookies (analytics only)

3. How long we keep your data

Data typeRetention period
CV and application data180 days from submission, then securely deleted
Contact / enquiry messages12 months
Immigration consultation notes12 months or as required by law
Cookie / analytics data13 months

Under GDPR, CVs are retained for a maximum of 180 days. After this, your data is permanently and securely deleted unless you give explicit consent for extended retention or a placement has occurred.

4. How we use your data

  • To match your profile to relevant job opportunities
  • To contact you about roles and career guidance
  • To provide immigration guidance and referrals
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies (required for site function) and, with your consent, analytics cookies. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

6. Your rights under GDPR

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability

To exercise any right, email [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share your CV with prospective employers only with your explicit consent. We may share limited data with immigration solicitor partners where you have requested a referral.

8. Contact & complaints

Data Controller: NK Recruitment, 19 Watergate Street, Navan, Co. Meath, Ireland. Email: [email protected]. You may also lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission at dataprotection.ie.
